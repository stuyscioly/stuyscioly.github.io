Our Sponsors




How You Can Contribute

We are Stuyvesant High School's Science Olympiad team. Science Olympiad is an organization with the goals of helping high school kids learn and love different fields of science, and has been the largest outlet for students to express their love for over ten years at Stuyvesant. We pride ourselves in being one of the top performing Science Olympiad teams in New York City.

As the Stuyvesant Science Olympiad team is student-run, hundreds and thousands of dollars are spent on registration fees, team activities, kits, and building material costs.



Sponsorship Levels

Contributor ($100-499): Donators will receive a letter of appreciation.

Bronze Sponsor ($500-999): Donators will receive customized Stuyvesant Science Olympiad pens and keychains.

Silver Sponsor ($1000-1999): Donators' websites will be featured on stuyoly.org.

Gold Sponsor ($2000-3999): Donators will be featured on the official Stuyvesant Science Olympiad apparel.

Platinum Sponsor ($4000+:) Donators will have the privilege of naming the machines, and will receive a plaque.
