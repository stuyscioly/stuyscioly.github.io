Earth Science

Our Earth Science division specializes in the very diverse Earth Science events, covering topics such as Oceanography, Geology, and Astronomy. Earth Science is perfect for people who are curious and willing to learn about macro processes of the Earth and beyond.

We're composed of four events: Astronomy, Dynamic Planet, Fossils, and Geologic Mapping.

Astronomy

This event allows you to learn about the many processes of the Universe and the physics behind them, from stellar formation to cosmology.

Dynamic Planet

This event delves into the geological processes of the Earth and its history.
