Biology

Our Biology division studies a broad variety of topics ranging from cells and microbial life to the biology of humans and reptiles to the study of populations.

We're composed of 5 events: Anatomy & Physiology, Disease Detectives, Water Quality, Herpetology and Designer Genes.

Anatomy & Physiology

This event focuses on the structure and function of specific body systems that will be tested. (This year: Cardiovascular, Lymphatic and Excretory)

  • Learn More

Disease Detectives

This event involves the study of epidemiology, such as how diseases can be transmitted across humans. Specific types of illnesses are rotated each year.

  • Learn More

Water Quality

This event involves the study of the interaction between marine organisms and their surroundings, with a focus on the impact of invasive species.
