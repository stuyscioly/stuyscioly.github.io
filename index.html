Who We Are

Science Olympiad is a prestigious nationwide competition that consists of twenty-three events that span a great number of scientific disciplines and branches, and Stuyvesant High School leads the pack with its determination and unrelenting drive for success. Known primarily for its competitive standards and challenging curriculum, Stuyvesant is striving to add another notch onto its belt by expanding into the Science Olympiad scene and dominating.

Every year, Stuyvesant consistently places within the top five ranks of New York City regionals and within the top ten ranks of New York State competitions.

...And that is just the beginning.

Never stopping short of perfect, the highly selective Stuyvesant Science Olympiad team continues to reach for national recognition at the Nationals level of competition.
